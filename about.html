Our Journey

From humble beginnings to Nairobi's trusted home essentials delivery service

HomeStock Express was born out of a simple need: to make home essentials easily accessible to busy Nairobi families. What started as a small operation with just 3 products and 1 delivery bike during the pandemic has grown into a trusted service delivering to thousands of happy customers.

2020

Humble Beginnings

Launched with just 3 essential products. Operated from a small apartment in Nairobi with one delivery bike. Served our first 50 customers during the pandemic.

2021

Rapid Growth

Expanded to 50+ products. Added M-Pesa payment integration. Reached 1,000+ customers. Expanded delivery area to cover all of Nairobi.

2022

Service Enhancement

Launched same-day delivery guarantee. Introduced mobile app. Partnered with 10+ local suppliers. Achieved 4.8/5 customer rating.

2023

Market Leadership

Served 15,000+ customers. Expanded team to 20+ employees. Introduced sustainable packaging. Won "Best Home Delivery Service" award.
