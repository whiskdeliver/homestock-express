Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver daily from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2:00 PM. Orders after 2:00 PM will be scheduled for next morning delivery.

How do I track my delivery?

After confirming your payment via WhatsApp (0789 50 3762), our delivery team will provide you with an estimated delivery time. You'll receive updates via WhatsApp as your order progresses through preparation and delivery stages.

Do you deliver on weekends and holidays?

Yes! We deliver 7 days a week, including weekends and most public holidays. Our delivery hours may be slightly adjusted on major holidays, which will be communicated in advance.

What if I'm not available during delivery?

Our delivery team will contact you 30 minutes before arrival. If you're unavailable, you can:

  • Specify a safe drop-off location
  • Authorize someone else to receive
  • Reschedule for a more convenient time
We offer one free rescheduling per order.

How are perishable items handled during delivery?

All perishable kitchen items are packed in insulated bags with ice packs to maintain freshness. Our delivery vehicles are equipped to handle temperature-sensitive products, ensuring they arrive in perfect condition.

What's your delivery radius?

We cover all major towns listed above. If your specific area isn't listed, please contact us at 0789 50 3762 to check availability. We're constantly expanding our delivery network!
